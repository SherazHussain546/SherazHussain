## 3. Group Rules

### 1. Prioritize Inclusion & Accessibility
We exist to help everyone. Do not mock or dismiss users who are not tech-savvy. Use clear language, avoid unnecessary jargon where possible, and be patient with those learning.

### 2. Open-Source Integrity
This group is for sharing and discussing free, open-source platforms and tools. No paywalled software, hidden subscriptions, or deceptive "freemium" models. We promote tools that are genuinely free for the community.

### 3. Professionalism & Mutual Respect
Maintain a high standard of professional discourse. Harassment, hate speech, or derogatory comments of any kind will result in an immediate ban. We engineer excellence through respectful collaboration.

### 4. Zero Spam Policy
No unsolicited self-promotion, affiliate links, or repetitive marketing posts. Sharing relevant open-source projects you’ve built or found is encouraged, but keep it high-value and mission-aligned.

### 5. Privacy & Data Security
Never share sensitive personal information, private API keys, or unsecured credentials in the group. We advocate for Zero Trust principles and robust digital integrity.

### 6. Constructive Feedback Only
When reviewing open-source tools shared by others, focus on technical improvement and user experience. Aim to elevate the project, not tear down the engineer.

### 7. No AI-Generated Low-Value Spam
While we love AI, please do not flood the group with low-effort, AI-generated "engagement bait" posts. Every contribution should add meaningful value to the community.
