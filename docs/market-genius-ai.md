# Case Study: Market Genius AI - Engineering Financial Intelligence

## Executive Summary
Market Genius is a high-fidelity financial analytics platform designed to bridge the gap between raw market volatility and strategic decision-making. By leveraging Generative AI and real-time data pipelines, the system provides users with actionable trade signals.

## Strategic Impact
*   **Accuracy Boost**: Increased trade signal precision by **40%** through advanced LLM sentiment analysis.
*   **Operational Velocity**: Reduced user setup and analysis time from **4 hours to 30 minutes**.
*   **Technical Integrity**: Maintained sub-100ms latency for real-time data ingestion from AlphaVantage and Crypto.com.

## Technical Deep Dive
The architecture utilizes **Next.js 15** for the frontend and **Google Genkit** for the AI reasoning layer. We implemented a "Signal Logic Architecture" that translates complex market indicators into human-readable strategy.
